About Code for Growth

Empowering young Rwandan innovators through technology and collaboration

Our Purpose

Code for Growth is a platform designed to nurture Rwanda's next generation of tech leaders. We believe in the power of coding, creativity, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving to drive meaningful change in our communities.

Our hackathon brings together Rwanda's brightest minds to collaborate on solutions that address real-world challenges in education, healthcare, agriculture, and digital transformation.

Our Vision

To make Rwanda a hub for technological innovation in Africa by empowering its youth.

Our Values

Innovation, Collaboration, Excellence, and Impact are at our core.

Event Phases

1. Application Phase

Participants submit their innovative ideas for review by our expert panel.

2. Semi-Finals

Shortlisted participants refine their ideas with mentorship support.

3. Grand Finals

Finalists present their solutions to judges for prizes and incubation opportunities.

Judging Criteria

Innovation (30%)

Originality and creativity of the proposed solution.

Impact (30%)

Potential positive effect on Rwanda's development goals.

Feasibility (20%)

Practical implementation within Rwanda's context.

Presentation (20%)

Clarity and effectiveness of the pitch.
